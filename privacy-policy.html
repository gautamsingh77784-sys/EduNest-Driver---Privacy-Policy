Privacy Policy

EduNest Driver helps authorized school van drivers share trip and location updates with the school transport system.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the EduNest Driver app used by authorized school van drivers. The app connects drivers with the EduNest school transport system for assigned van, route, trip, and student transport workflows.

The school or institution manages driver accounts and transport records. The school may also have its own policies for employee, contractor, transport, and student safety records.

2. Data We Collect

Depending on the features enabled by the school, EduNest Driver may process:

  • Driver account information such as name, phone number, login identifier, role, school name, and authentication session data.
  • Assigned van and route information such as vehicle plate number, route name, assigned students, trip state, and dashboard status.
  • Location information such as latitude, longitude, timestamp, and online or offline tracking status.
  • Device and technical data such as app settings, saved login state, API requests, errors, and notification permission status.

3. Location Data

EduNest Driver uses location permission to send van location updates to the school transport system while tracking or trip features are active. Location may be used in the foreground and, where permitted by the device and school policy, in the background for active transport tracking.

Location data is used for school transport safety, route visibility, trip monitoring, and parent or admin transport updates where enabled by the school.

4. How Data Is Used

EduNest Driver uses data to:

  • Authenticate authorized drivers and load the assigned school transport dashboard.
  • Start and end trips, mark driver availability, and update van tracking status.
  • Show assigned van, route, students, and latest saved coordinates.
  • Send van location updates to the EduNest backend for transport monitoring.
  • Maintain service reliability, security, diagnostics, and support.

5. Sharing

Driver and transport information may be visible to authorized school administrators, transport staff, and parents or guardians where the school enables live van tracking. Data may also be processed by service providers that support hosting, APIs, maps, notifications, diagnostics, and infrastructure.

EduNest Driver does not sell driver or location data.

6. Security

The app uses authenticated API access and local app storage for session state. Users should keep their login details private, protect their device, and report any lost device or suspicious account activity to the school administrator immediately.

7. Choices and Controls

You can manage location permission from your device settings. If location permission is disabled, active van tracking and trip workflows may not work correctly.

For driver account deletion or access removal, see the EduNest Driver Account Deletion page.

8. Retention

Driver, trip, route, and location records may be retained by the school for transport operations, safety, attendance, audit, dispute resolution, legal compliance, and service reliability. Retention periods may vary by school policy and applicable law.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact your school administrator or EduNest transport administrator. Include your name, school name, registered phone or login identifier, and a clear description of the request.
